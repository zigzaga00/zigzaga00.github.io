about our

mission

Girls are brilliant at computing — creative, intelligent, and full of amazing ideas. But girls only make up 17% of A-level Computing students! The Sisters in2 Tech are here to change that! In our sessions, girls take the lead on projects that matter to them - learning in a safe, supportive space - following their interests together and growing in confidence as digital creators and changemakers.

welcome aboard

Join Us

Come and Join the Sisters In2 Tech — a fun, free and friendly computing club for girls only where every girl is welcome, no matter how much (or how little!) experience she has.

We meet Sunday afternoons from 2 - 4pm at the Tiddington Community Centre. Girls aged 7 and up can bring a laptop or tablet if they have one — but don't worry if not! You can easily share with others or use a club device.

So, if you love making things, enjoy being creative with computers, or want to have fun with tech — all while making new friends in a relaxed and supportive space — you are welcome to join us!

explore our

heritage

barbara rogoff

Barbara Rogoff shows us that learning doesn't happen in isolation—it happens through shared experiences, in real communities, and with purpose. With the Sisters In2 Tech, her ideas come to life as children learn side by side, helping one another. Here, learning is collaborative, empowering, and rooted in the real world.

read more

maria montessori

Maria Montessori believed that children thrive when they are free to follow their curiosity in a carefully prepared, respectful environment. Her approach inspires the Sisters In2 Tech to be a focussed, creative space where learners choose their own paths and work at their own pace. We believe that with trust and freedom, all children flourish.

read more

grace hopper

Grace Hopper was a visionary computer scientist who believed that thinking differently and breaking limits were the keys to progress. The Sisters In2 Tech follow her example: encouraging bold ideas, supporting fearless experimentation, and championing female inclusion to spark change, open doors and empower girls to shape the future.
